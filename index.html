Profish is a campaign that aims to protect and preserve marine life by eliminating waste material found in the ocean. For each login, one kilogram of trash is removed from the ocean and is upcycled via recycling factories into something useful. According to an international study, the Philippines topped as ocean polluters, with about 3.3 kilograms of trash per person each year. With this, our campaign on a bigger scale is expected to help with this issue in the country, and even worldwide.
